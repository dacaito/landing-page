## **HOW IT WORKS**

### **Automated verification at the point of dispatch**

Vexgen AI verifies every outbound shipment before it leaves the site.

1. **Capture the loaded truck**

Cameras monitor the truck at the dispatch point. 

1. **Detect and count what is loaded**

Vision-based models detect and count loaded materials directly from the truck.

1. **Compare against the shipment order**

The system matches detected quantities with the expected shipment from the ERP.

1. **Approve or block dispatch**

If everything matches, the truck is cleared.

If not, the shipment is flagged and held before leaving the plant.

No scanners.

No tags.

No manual checks.
