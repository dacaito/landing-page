About Vixgen

Vixgen was created to address a persistent and costly problem in industrial operations: the gap between ERP inventory records and physical reality.

In manufacturing and warehouse environments, inventory data errors accumulate quietly. Over time, this leads to stockouts, excess buffers, write-offs, and operational inefficiencies that are difficult to trace and even harder to eliminate.

Vixgen focuses on restoring trust in operational data by making physical inventory movements continuously observable and verifiable.

Built for real industrial operations

Vixgen is designed specifically for legacy industrial environments, where:

  • Operations run continuously
  • Variability is unavoidable
  • Manual controls do not scale
  • Systems must be reliable, not experimental

The product is built with production constraints in mind, prioritizing accuracy, robustness, and minimal disruption.
