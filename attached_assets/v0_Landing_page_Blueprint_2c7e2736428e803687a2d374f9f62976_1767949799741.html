THE PROBLEM

Phantom inventory is invisible — until it becomes expensive.

Manufacturing and warehouse operations rely on ERP data that often does not reflect what actually exists on the shop floor.

This gap quietly creates:

  • Stockouts and emergency purchases
  • Excess inventory and write-offs
  • Hours lost on manual recounts
  • Decisions made with unreliable data

ERP systems are only as good as the physical data they receive.

Today, that data is incomplete.

Continuous reconciliation between physical reality and ERP

Vexgen AI connects physical movements to digital records automatically.

  2. Validate inventory events

    Vision-based intelligence identifies materials, movements and quantities.

  3. Synchronize ERP in real time

    Discrepancies are detected and corrected automatically, without operator input.
